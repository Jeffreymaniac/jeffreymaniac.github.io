List of website's 404 pages

This is a list of some website's 404 pages. If you don't know what a 404 page is, then a 404 page is a page that websites use to tell you that the page you tried to go to doesn't exist.

  • Minecraft.net 404: https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/error/404
  • Google.com 404: https://google.com/404.html
  • Scratch.mit.edu 404: https://scratch.mit.edu/404/
  • Scratch.mit.edu 403: https://scratch.mit.edu/404.html (pretty funny going to 404.html gives a 403 but just /404 is a normal 404)
  • Pokefarm.com 404: https://pokefarm.com/404/

  • Microsoft.com 404: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/404
